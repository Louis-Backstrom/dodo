# Slender-billed Curlew surveys

Slender-billed Curlew (*Numenius tenuirostris*) survey data from
Buchanan et al. (2025). The temporal range (`init.time` to `test.time`)
of these data is 1892 to 2022.

## Usage

``` r
curlew_surveys
```

## Format

a `data.frame` with 11 columns and 206 rows.

## References

**Key Reference**

Buchanan, G. M., Chapple, B., Berryman, A. J., Crockford, N., Jansen, J.
J. F. J., & Bond, A. L. (2025). Global extinction of Slender-billed
Curlew (*Numenius tenuirostris*). *Ibis*, 167(2), 357-370.
[doi:10.1111/ibi.13368](https://doi.org/10.1111/ibi.13368)
