# Slender-billed Curlew sightings (raw)

Unprocessed Slender-billed Curlew (*Numenius tenuirostris*) sighting
record from Buchanan et al. (2025).

## Usage

``` r
curlew_raw
```

## Format

a `data.frame` with 6 columns and 1174 rows.

## References

**Key Reference**

Buchanan, G. M., Chapple, B., Berryman, A. J., Crockford, N., Jansen, J.
J. F. J., & Bond, A. L. (2025). Global extinction of Slender-billed
Curlew (*Numenius tenuirostris*). *Ibis*, 167(2), 357-370.
[doi:10.1111/ibi.13368](https://doi.org/10.1111/ibi.13368)
